<p><i>ADLMIDI Player</i> a MIDI-player based on emulator of a Frequency Modulation chip Yamaha OPL3. This small MIDI-player made with using of <a href='https://github.com/Wohlstand/libADLMIDI/' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>libADLMIDI</a> library - a fork of origina ADLMIDI a console program implementation.</p><p>The app a.o. features stereo sound, OPL3 emulation with four-operator mode support, FM patches from a number of known PC games, MIDI, RMI and Wolfinstein 3D IMF files support and more. For additional details, head over to their source repository.</p>
